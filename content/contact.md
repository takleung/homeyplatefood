# Contact Us

Have a question, feedback, or a delicious recipe to share? We'd love to hear from you!

## Submit Your Recipe

We're always looking for high-quality recipes from home cooks and food enthusiasts. If you have a recipe you're proud of, please share it with our community.

### Recipe Submission Guidelines

- **Original or Traditional**: Share your original creations or family recipes (with proper attribution)
- **Tested**: Recipe should be tested and produce consistent results
- **Detailed**: Include prep time, cook time, difficulty level, and step-by-step instructions
- **Photos**: High-quality photos are highly encouraged (but not required)
- **Measurements**: Please provide precise measurements

### What to Include

1. **Recipe Name**: A clear, descriptive title
2. **Description**: Brief overview of the dish (2-3 sentences)
3. **Cuisine & Category**: What type of dish is it?
4. **Ingredients**: Complete list with measurements
5. **Instructions**: Step-by-step cooking directions
6. **Tips**: Any pro tips, variations, or substitutions
7. **Photos**: Optional but appreciated

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take to review submitted recipes?**
A: We typically review submissions within 5-7 business days.

**Q: Will I be credited for my recipe?**
A: Absolutely! All published recipes include proper attribution to the contributor.

**Q: Can I submit recipes from other cuisines?**
A: Yes! We celebrate all cuisines and cooking styles from around the world.

**Q: Do you accept video recipes?**
A: Currently, we focus on written recipes with photos, but we're exploring video content for the future.

**Q: Can I suggest improvements to existing recipes?**
A: Yes! We welcome constructive feedback and recipe variations.
