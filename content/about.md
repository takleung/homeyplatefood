# About Homey Plate Food

Welcome to our gallery of homemade culinary delights!

This gallery showcases various dishes, recipes, and food photography. Each album represents different categories of food, cooking styles, or special occasions.

## How to Use This Gallery

- Browse through different albums on the homepage
- Click on any album to view the full gallery
- Click on individual photos to see them in full screen with the lightbox viewer
- Use the arrow keys or swipe gestures to navigate between photos

Enjoy your visit!
